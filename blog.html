Winter Safety Tips: Preventing Slips and Falls on Icy Calgary Surfaces

Calgary's harsh winters create hazardous conditions that demand vigilant safety measures. With annual snowfall averaging 128.8 cm and frequent freeze-thaw cycles, property owners must implement comprehensive prevention strategies. Essential practices include regular snow removal, proper de-icing application, and consistent surface monitoring. Professional snow removal services offer expertise, proper equipment, and liability protection. Property owners must clear sidewalks within 24 hours of snowfall and maintain safe access. Pedestrians should wear appropriate footwear and walk cautiously. Emergency response planning, including maintaining contact information for services and documenting incidents, is crucial for winter safety management.
