Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our snow removal services

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

We prioritize rapid response times and typically arrive within 2-4 hours of your request during normal conditions. For emergency situations, we can often respond within 1-2 hours, depending on weather conditions and current demand.

Do you offer seasonal contracts?

Yes, we offer both seasonal contracts and one-time service options to meet your needs. Seasonal contracts provide priority service, discounted rates, and guaranteed availability throughout the winter season.

What areas do you service in Calgary?

We service all areas within Calgary and surrounding communities including Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, and Okotoks. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured with comprehensive liability coverage. We carry both general liability and property damage insurance to protect our clients and their property.
